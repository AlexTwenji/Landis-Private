Activities

Here at Landis Private, we aim to provide your with the most condusive environment to maximise your vacation, holiday and/or your overall stay with us. We have a wide range of Activities including, boat-riding, swimming (with humans, sharks and/or dolphins), snorkeling, jet-sking and surfboarding among other activities. For further indulgences, we provide a pool-side bar, a beach bar and a docked ship bar (strictly for persons over the age of 18 years).
