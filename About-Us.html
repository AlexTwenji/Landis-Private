About Us

We are an Island Resort based in Watamu, Kenya. We boast sandy beaches with a state-of-the-art conferencing facility, luxurious holiday homes, dinning options, swimming, fitness, spa, boat riding, bird watching and an unmatched concierge service. From outdoor recreation to ocean-view cocktails; swimming to sunbathing; casual dining to waterfront entertainment; our resort hotel is the perfect place to rest, play, and make new memories of a lifetime.

The Island Resort opened its doors to the public on August 1995 and has maintained its position as a world leader and pioneer in the luxury resort sector.
